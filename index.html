Central Vaccum Cleaning

Central Vacuum Cleaner (also known as a built-in vacuum) is a type of vacuum appliance,installed into a house or office as a semi-permanent ductwork fixture. Central vacuum systems are designed to remove dust, dirt, and debris from homes and buildings, sending these harmful particles through a duct system installed inside the walls to a collection container in designated utility space.
